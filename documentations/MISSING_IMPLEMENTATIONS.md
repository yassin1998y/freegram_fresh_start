This document lists potentially missing features that could enhance the social feed ecosystem.

## üìå Content Management & Organization

### 1. Saved Posts / Bookmarks System
- **Save posts for later viewing**
  - Save button on each post
  - Saved posts collection/page
  - Organize saved posts into collections/folders
  - Private saved posts (only visible to user)
  - Quick access from profile menu

### 2. Post Drafts
- **Save posts as drafts**
  - Auto-save draft while creating post
  - Drafts list/manager
  - Edit/delete drafts
  - Resume creating from draft
  - Multiple drafts support

### 3. Post Scheduling
- **Schedule posts for future publication**
  - Schedule date/time picker
  - Scheduled posts queue
  - Edit scheduled posts before publication
  - Cancel scheduled posts
  - Notification when scheduled post goes live

### 4. Post Archiving
- **Archive old posts (hide from profile but keep)**
  - Archive button on posts
  - Archived posts section
  - Unarchive functionality
  - Keep engagement stats on archived posts

### 5. Post Pinning
- **Pin posts to top of profile**
  - Pin post option (limit 3-5 pinned posts)
  - Pinned posts appear first on profile
  - Reorder pinned posts
  - Unpin functionality
